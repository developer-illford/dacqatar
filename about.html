About DAC

Dubai Based Advertising Services Company

Established in 2015, a leading signage and fit-out company dedicated to transforming spaces & brands. With a passion for innovation, design excellence, and meticulous craftsmanship, We create captivating environments that leave a lasting impression. Our integrated approach seamlessly blends signage expertise with interior design, Ensuring cohesive & impactful solutions for a diverse range of clients.

Our Mission

At DAC our mission is to elevate spaces through innovative design, meticulous planning, and expert execution. We aim to exceed our clients' expectations by delivering exceptional solutions that enhance both aesthetics and functionality, while also adhering to deadlines and budgets.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every space and brand is transformed through cutting-edge design and exceptional craftsmanship. We strive to lead the industry with innovative solutions that inspire, captivate, and exceed expectations, shaping impactful environments that leave a lasting impression.

Why Choose Us

Excellence: Our team of skilled designers & architects blend creativity with functionality to deliver designs that captivate and inspire.

Craftsmanship: We take pride in our meticulous attention to detail and skilled craftsmanship, ensuring the highest quality in every project.

Innovation: Staying ahead of industry trends, we incorporate innovative materials and technologies to provide cutting-edge solutions.

Collaborative Approach: We believe in fostering strong client relationships through open communication, collaboration, and a deep understanding of your vision.

Turnkey Solutions: From concept to installation, we offer end-to-end solutions, streamlining the process and ensuring a seamless experience.

Sustainability: We are committed to environmentally responsible practices, integrating sustainable materials and practices into our projects.

Efficient Inhouse Capability: With sophisticated machineries and infrastructure, we are able handle various tasks, functions, or processes internally, without relying heavily on external resources or outsourcing.

Timely Delivery: We prioritize meeting deadlines and managing schedules efficiently, ensuring that your project is completed on time without compromising on quality.
